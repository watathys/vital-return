A Deeper Dive Into Recovery

Critical Protein Replenishment

A single plasma donation removes a significant amount of protein from your body, which is the primary cause of post-donation fatigue. Vital Return is uniquely formulated with a high-quality, easily digestible protein blend to give your body the essential building blocks needed to synthesize new plasma proteins. This is our key differentiator and something that water or typical sports drinks simply cannot provide.

Advanced Rehydration

Dehydration after a donation can lead to dizziness, headaches, and lightheadedness. Effective rehydration requires electrolytes to help your body absorb and retain fluid in the bloodstream. Our scientifically-balanced electrolyte blend accelerates this process, helping you restore proper fluid balance much more efficiently than by drinking water alone.

Immediate Energy Restoration

The donation process is physically demanding. To combat the lethargic feeling that follows, our formula includes a source of clean, easily-metabolized carbohydrates. This provides an immediate source of fuel to stabilize blood sugar levels, restore your energy, and empower you to get back to your classes, work, or life without missing a beat.
