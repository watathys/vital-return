Common Donor Questions

When should I drink this?

For best results, mix one packet with 16oz of cold water and drink it immediately after your donation (within 15 minutes). This helps prevent the post-donation crash.

What are the key ingredients in Vital Return?

Our scientifically-formulated blend focuses on the three critical components of post-donation recovery: 1. Protein to rebuild lost plasma proteins, 2. A robust electrolyte blend for rapid rehydration, and 3. Natural energy to combat post-donation fatigue.

How quickly will my body replace the plasma I lost?

Your body is incredibly efficient. The water component of your plasma is generally replaced within 24 hours with proper hydration. The proteins and other vital nutrients take longer to fully regenerate, which is why consistent, targeted nutrition is crucial for regular donors.
